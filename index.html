Early childhood development training

Early Childhood Development (ECD) is an overall development of children from birth to nine years of age, which lays a foundation for a child’s cognitive, emotional, social and physical potential.  Brain development of a child is part of his or her cognitive development which determines a child’s ability to think, attitudes and understanding of the world around the child. ECD practitioners provide children with education between reception year to grade 3 in order to assist them lay  a good  foundation for further learning in their lives.

About Leseding Skills Development Centre

Leseding Computer Systems t/a Leseding Skills Development Centre (LSDC) is 100% black woman owned private company and an accredited Private FET Training Provider. It was registered as a close corporation in 2004 and it is level 1 BEE contributor with 135% procurement recognition.

Initially the primary focus of the company was basic computer literacy training and it was then known as Leseding Computer Training but has extended its training scope to Information Technology full qualifications, Wholesale and Retail Operations programmes, Consumer Co-operative development, management programmes and Industrial training.

In its establishment LSDC has been committed to Skills Development and Empowerment, hence it seeks not only to be an accredited Training Provider, but also be a potential Employer by contributing to job creation, through its strategic networks.

Many skills development and job placement focus has been on HDI (Historically Disadvantaged Individuals) according to Employment Equity Act chapter 2.1- Affirmative Action Programme. Whereas as an accredited training provider LSDC plays a big role in training disadvantaged individuals in the implementation of Township Economy Programmes that are based on the Retail Sector and Social Development

About Leseding Skills Development Center

Leseding Computer Systems is trading as Leseding Skills Development.It is about the provision of training and skillsdevelopment programmes.Offering ranges from basic learning to advanced learning.

Any person, body, organization, business and institution, who has a need for effectiveness and efficiency, therefore will at one point or another require knowledge, skills and a programme that will best suit and meet those needs.

LSDC is geared towards addressing these needs through a variety of programmes, the details and specifics of these programmes depend on the specific requirement of each individual or company.

Vision

To become a responsive capacity development ETD service provider that builds a skilled and capable workforce that shares in, and contributes to, the benefits and opportunities of economic expansion.

Mission

To increase access to high quality and relevant education and training and skills development opportunities, including workplace learning and experience, to enable effective participation in the economy and society by all South Africans and reduce inequalities.

Business Objectives and Goals

  • To provide credit bearing learning programmes.
  • To contribute to the provision of skilled workforce to employers.
  • To participate in skills development through strategic partnerships.
  • To become the leader in skills development interventions.
  • To maintain and comply with accreditation standards.
  • To remain profitable and sustainable training provider.

Accreditation

LSDCis accredited by MICT SETA, Wholesale and Retail Setaand ETDP Seta. Currently its assessors and moderators are accredited. Its training materialsare also aligned to the SAQA NQF (National Qualification Framework) standards and approved by relevant Seta. Accreditation
